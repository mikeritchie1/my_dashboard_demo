About

Directed: Antoine Fuqua (Training Day).
Starring: Jaafar Jackson, Nia Long, Miles Teller, Colman Domingo.

Witness his rise. Experience his genius. Discover his legend.

From the producer of Bohemian Rhapsody, comes Michael, a musical biopic about the life and legacy of Michael Jackson, one of the most influential artists the world has ever known.
The film traces his journey as the lead of the Jackson Five to the visionary artist who became the biggest entertainer in the world. Highlighting both his life off-stage and some of his most iconic performances, the film gives audiences a front-row seat to Michael Jackson as never before.
